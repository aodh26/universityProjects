Instructions:

Controls:

  • You can move with the arrow keys or by pressing the W(up), A(left), S(right) and D(down) keys if you are left handed.
  • Press the space bar to drop a bomb!

Aim:

  • You have 30 seconds to get as many points as possible!
  • The rooms are randomised every time you play. A new experience every time!
  • Speed and accuracy are the names of the game!
  • Touching a wall will kill you! they're covered in ectoplasm!
  • You get points from blowing up Ghosts or going through doorways.
  • You get more points from going through doorways than you do for blowing up Ghosts.

Baddies:

There are two types of baddies:

Ghosts

  • Ghosts can walk through walls and follow the player.
  • They do 25 damage when they touch the player and also knock the player back.
  • You get points if you blow them up with a Bomb!
  • Be Careful! Don't get knocked into a wall!

Lizards

  • Lizards walk around randomly and do not follow the player.
  • But be careful, they're immune to bombs and kill in one hit!

Bombs:

  • Bombs can be dropped by pressing space and take 1 second to explode.
  • Don't worry though, you're wearing an anti-bomb suit!
